Topic name: Nanotechnology in relation to medicine.
Description and why it is important
Topic Description Nanotechnology is a fairly new technology that centers upon the ability to manipulate things in the atomic scale. Within current day medicine it can be used to help treat diseases like cancer, tuberculosis, and other internal worries like blood clots. In the future it is speculated that nanotechnology within medicine, or nanomedicine, will be able to help society not only improve our health, but also monitor and prevent potential lethal diseases from invading out immune systems.
